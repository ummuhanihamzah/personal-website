MY FAMILY

My family has always been my strength. I have parents. My mother is a housewife and my father is a retired soldier. My mother is the strongest woman I have ever seen in my life. She is a super woman. She is a fine individual with poise and dignity. My father is a man of strong principles. His presence at home itself gives us a sense of security and hope. He is very gentle, disciplined and strict. I also have 3 brothers. My eldest brother is a soldier. He is strict, loving and caring brother. My second brother is a counselor. He is funny, cheerful and loving brother. My third brother is a businessman. He is rather quite, caring and loving brother.I also have a sister. She is a very soft and gentle girl. She is caring and loving sister.
